ABOUT THE CURRICULUM

This is basically an attempt to introduce one to the History of Philosophy, as well as bringing it together in dialogue with contemporary debates issues.

The contemporary debates revolve around 2 topics: (1) on the concrete everyday end, we will be dealing with the question of AI Ethics and technology in general, and (2) on the abstract philosophical end, we will be dealing with what could be considered as the Deleuze/Lacan debate, which is one of the big ongoing discourses in the online space.

At the end of the course, there will be a presentation of a final project, basically doing something creative with what you've learned in this course. There is no criteria or rubric as to what to make other than try not to make it something that looks like it took only 1 day.
